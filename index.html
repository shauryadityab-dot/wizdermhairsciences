Our Core Procedures

Explore our advanced, carefully vetted treatments designed to restore your hair naturally with maximum efficiency.

Hair transplantation is the most definitive solution for baldness. We utilize the latest Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) techniques to extract individual hair follicles from the donor area and meticulously implant them into the balding regions.

Our surgeon-led approach ensures absolute precision in graft angle and depth, delivering a perfectly natural-looking hairline with maximum density and minimal scarring.

PRP Therapy is a non-surgical marvel that uses your body's own healing mechanism. We draw a small amount of your blood, centrifuge it to isolate the growth factor-rich plasma, and micro-inject it into the scalp.

These growth factors stimulate dormant hair follicles, increase blood supply to the hair roots, and significantly boost hair thickness and volume. It's an excellent standalone treatment for early thinning or combined with transplants for accelerated healing.

Growth Factor Concentrate is the next evolution in regenerative hair therapy. Unlike traditional PRP, GFC uses highly concentrated, acellular growth factors extracted from your platelets.

This results in a purer, more potent serum that delivers remarkable results in arresting hair fall, rejuvenating dying follicles, and promoting robust new growth without the inflammation sometimes associated with RBCs or WBCs in standard PRP.
